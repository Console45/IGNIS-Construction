A Few Words About US

IGNIS Engineering and Construction is a Ghanaian owned company that introduces innovative, efficient and cost-effective building technologies into the Ghanaian space. IGNIS has come up with innovative housing designs that are eco-friendly and cover lest cost in its build. This has become necessary considering the huge housing deficit in the Ghanaian and African setting and also due to the contribution of the construction sector to carbon emission through non eco-friendly buildings. The world has reached its point where it requires swift and thoughtful considerations to help reduce the effects of Global warming through our lifestyles.
